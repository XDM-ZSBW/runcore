# Grandma Herrman's Chili

*The real one. Not the one she gave the church cookbook.*

## Ingredients
- 2 lbs ground beef (80/20)
- 1 large onion, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 cans kidney beans (dark red), drained
- 1 can black beans, drained
- 2 cans diced tomatoes (do NOT drain)
- 1 small can tomato paste
- 2 tbsp chili powder
- 1 tbsp cumin
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1/2 tsp cayenne (Margaret uses 1/4 tsp)
- Salt & pepper to taste
- 1 bottle beer (whatever Robert has open)

## Instructions
1. Brown the beef in a big pot. Don't drain the fat — that's the flavor.
2. Add onion and garlic, cook until soft (5 min).
3. Add everything else. Stir.
4. Simmer on low for at least 2 hours. 3 is better.
5. Serve with cornbread. Not crackers. Cornbread.

## Notes
- Freezes great. Makes enough for 3 meals.
- Robert likes it with sharp cheddar and raw onion on top.
- For the church version, skip the beer and halve the cayenne.
