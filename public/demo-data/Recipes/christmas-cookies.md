# Margaret's Buckeye Cookies

*Every Christmas since 1982. The kids fight over these.*

## Ingredients
- 1.5 cups creamy peanut butter
- 1 cup butter, softened
- 3.5 cups powdered sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
- 2 tbsp shortening

## Instructions
1. Mix peanut butter, butter, sugar, and vanilla until smooth
2. Roll into 1-inch balls
3. Stick a toothpick in each, freeze 30 min
4. Melt chocolate chips + shortening (double boiler or microwave)
5. Dip each ball 3/4 into chocolate, leave the top exposed (looks like a buckeye)
6. Set on wax paper, remove toothpick, smooth the hole
7. Refrigerate until chocolate sets

## Notes
- Makes about 60 cookies
- Hide a batch before the party or there won't be any left
- Lisa's kids are allergic to peanuts — make a separate batch with sunbutter
