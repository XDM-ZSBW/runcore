## Banned words and patterns

- **Banned words:** Add words the user never wants (e.g. "leverage," "synergy," "disrupt"). Check every draft.
- **Banned openings:** e.g. "In today's world," "It's no secret that."
- **Structural traps:** Avoid forced rule-of-three, copula-heavy sentences, excessive hedging. Max one em-dash per paragraph unless the user prefers more.

## Signature patterns

- How does the user like to open sentences? (e.g. leading with insight, question, or fact)
- How do they close? (e.g. call to action, question, or summary)

## Voice checkpoints (every ~500 words when drafting)

- Am I leading with insight?
- Am I being specific (numbers, examples)?
- Would the user actually post/say this?
